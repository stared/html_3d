Web is a great medium to express thoughts, feelings and impressions. But, as you surely have noticed, it often lacks... depth.

And to be honest, flat things are not cool. Greeks made Earth a sphere (later deformed by more accurate measurement). Geeks made 3D games. Einstein made spacetime curved. Adolescence makes... never mind.

So, please keep your eyes crossed and proceed to experience Web in 3D.

It should work without any special equipment, and even without a Facebook account (thanks to hardware in your brain; if it does not work for you, I cannot help).

It's cool, isn't it?


Also, it works great for data.
And for slacking.
And games.
And code, and math (but not in this alpha-decay release).
